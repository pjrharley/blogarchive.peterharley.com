Last night in LA

Hi,

It’s our last night in LA, although we’ve got most of tomorrow here too.

As planned we went to the Griffith Observatory on Saturday, up on the hills above LA. There was a lovely view across town but it was a little cloudy for photos! The observatory was good, there was a variety of sciency things there for Becky to not understand! We watched a couple of spacey shows there too, including a very cheesy demonstration of how to make your own comet.

Hello from LA

Hi all,

First post while we’re away. We’ve been in LA for a few days now and have been reasonably busy.

We arrived at our hostel on Tuesday afternoon after an 11 hour flight at around half 5 in the evening (half 1 in the morning UK time!). The flight was OK, originally we weren’t seated together but BA managed to move us (twice!). There were some amazing views from the plane over Hudson Bay and other bits of Canada and the US too.
