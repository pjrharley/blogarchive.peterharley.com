T minus 140 hours...

Just a quick post to say this is where we hope to be putting photos and blog posts while we are away, assuming we ever have time!

We’re off on Tuesday at midday, and currently are without passports! Hopefully this will be sorted out tomorrow…

Our current flight itinerary looks like this:

  • 6th April - Heathrow to Los Angeles Intl.
  • 13th April - LA to Nadi, Fiji
  • 2nd May - Fiji to Christ Church, New Zealand
  • 3rd June - Auckland to Syndey, Australia
  • 14th July - Cairnes to Singapore
  • 14th September - Bangkok, Thailand to Dubai
  • 17th September - Dubai to Heathrow

So add to your bookmarks: our travel posts or the feed for them.

Update: Passports arrived this morning so I guess we’re going then!
